The Hardware

Get an Android smartphone from eBay in used condition. Deliver it to an anonymous address that cannot be linked to your personal life (if possible). Choose a supported device from the LineageOS wiki

I can recommend the Fairphone models, and the Nexus or Google Pixel variants, because they're easily repairable when they break (see YouTube teardown videos for more on this).

Some Xiaomi Redmi devices are nice as well, but it takes up to 8 weeks to activate/unlock their Bootloader. Forget the Pinephone, because as of now (mid 2023) GLUDroid isn't there yet.

Get an anonymous Prepaid card or use Wi-Fi only for internet access. Anonymous prepaid cards are hard to get within the European Union, but there are some providers that allow to get them still in a pre-registered condition; so you don't have to link your passport to the SIM card.

Use a trashmail provider (like mytrashmail or similar), most throwaway mail providers are useless because the registration process takes days/weeks sometimes. Use a domain that doesn't look obvious to a human. If it's something like @throwaway123mail123.com a human will immediately recognize it and block/flag your account.

Some phone shops have preregistered SIM cards. Just ask them nicely :)

The Software

Follow the installation instructions in the LineageOS wiki for your selected device. Flash TWRP first, then an official LineageOS build from their website (e.g. via ADB sideload).

DONT EVER USE any XDA-developers provided builds, they're known to contain a lot of malware and banking trojans. Especially from our neighbors living in the friendly mother Russia.

Only use a LineageOS build, and only the one without the gapps. This will disable Google Play framework and you'll have to use F-Droid as an App store, but we don't want spyware anyways, right?

Never install SuperSU or Magisk on your phone. You don't need a rooted phone. Seriously, it's a big security risk and you're best to avoid it.

A lot of spyware (e.g. Pegasus and Predator) have support for exploiting the su command, and then they have full access for free, because you handed it over to them.

  1. Install F-Droid (Open Source App Store)
  2. Install NetGuard Firewall and give the developer a few bucks for it at netguard.me cause he's a nice guy
  3. Install Fennec (Open Source Firefox build)
  4. Inside Fennec Add-Ons, install uBlock Origin and LocalCDN
  5. Inside the uBlock Origin Settings, synchronize/download the newest filter lists
  6. Install AppWarden (apk) and use it to scan/audit/remove all your Apps for/from known Trackers

Operational Security / OPSEC

  • Never use any Social Media on your anonymous phone.
  • Never add any family or real-life contacts on your phone.
  • Never store any password on your mobile phone.
  • Treat your phone as a throwaway device, always.
  • Always use your phone in airplane mode, only activate SIM card when necessary.
  • Never deactivate airplane mode when you're closeby related ones or friends.
  • Register and use the phone's SIM card (aka not in airplane mode) only inside the forest/jungle somewhere far away (min 20km, better 70km) from your home.
  • Never connect the phone to another computer via USB.
  • Never enable ADB (Android debug bridge) cause malware sure likes to spread via ADB.
  • If you have the option, use OpenWRT on your router at home, and use a separated guest Wi-Fi for your smartphone.

Stay safe. You're not alone in this fight.
